Game Description

THE HOSTEL is a gripping GW-BASIC text adventure game divided into two thrilling parts:

Part One: The Mental Hostel

You find yourself thrown into a sinister mental hostel, condemned to spend your life there. Your mission is clear: find a way out at all costs. This place is not just a hospital; it's a sanity and health killer. The other residents serve as a grim reminder of your possible fate - some have lost their minds, others their lives. It's a loony bin prison, and your survival depends on your wits and determination.

Part Two: Alien Abduction

Just when you thought things couldn't get stranger, you're abducted by a UFO! Your consciousness is transferred into an alien body, leaving you disoriented and far from home. Now, you must navigate this alien environment, find a way to make the UFO land back on Earth, and return to your original body. Your ultimate goal: escape back to Earth and regain your human form.
